Common Questions

FAQ

What's included in the base price?

Every project includes custom design, responsive development, basic SEO setup, browser testing, and a handoff. We don't use templates — everything is built from scratch for you.

Do you require a deposit?

We require a 50% deposit of the setup fee to begin the work. The remaining 50% is due upon completion before the final files are delivered. The annual subscription will also be invoiced at this time too.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely. If the calculator shows more than you can afford, let's talk. We can often adjust scope or phase the project to fit your budget while still delivering something great.

Do you offer hosting?

We're partnered with some great web hosts, we can manage hosting for you, or hand everything off to your own hosting provider. We'll recommend the best option based on your project needs.

How accurate is the pricing estimate?

Pretty accurate! And we're committed to our prices. If complexity arises due to unforeseen ideas or developments, we'll be able to negotiate win wins the whole way through.

Do you use AI?

Depends on the project - but yes, AI tools have become a big part of the workflow. They save time (and money) on monotonous tasks so we can focus on the big goals and personalised designs.

Why shouldn't I just use AI to make my site for me?

AI tools are excellent for project development - but without a craftsman, they're useless. The importance of the designer's oversight on a project can't be overstated. The investment in someone who cares will stretch further than any AI tool out there.

Ready to Build Something Together?

I'd love to hear about your project. No pressure, no hard sell — just a conversation about what you're trying to achieve.
