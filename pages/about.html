About Sidequest

One Person. Infinite Care.

I'm Joel — a solo developer based in New Zealand who believes every organisation deserves digital tools built specifically for them, not adapted from templates made for someone else.

Let's Talk View My Work
Based in Christchurch, NZ
"I don't build websites. I build the thing that makes someone say 'finally, this is exactly what we needed.'"

— My working philosophy

Years ago, I watched a small community organisation struggle with a website that didn't fit them. They'd paid an agency thousands for something "professional" — and ended up with a template that looked like every other template on the internet.

That experience stuck with me. Not because the agency did anything wrong — they delivered exactly what they were hired to deliver. But because what they delivered wasn't actually what the organisation needed.

That's why I started Sidequest Digital. The name itself is a nod to how I see these projects — not as mass-produced main quests, but as unique sidequests that deserve their own attention, their own story, their own solution.

What I Believe

Guiding Principles

01

Clarity Over Complexity

The best solutions feel simple to use, even when they're technically sophisticated. I build things that just work — no training manuals required.

02

Your Story, Not Mine

Every pixel, word, and interaction should reflect who you are. I'm here to amplify your identity, not impose my aesthetic.

03

Built to Last

No trendy frameworks that'll be obsolete in two years. I choose technologies that are stable, maintainable, and won't leave you stranded.

04

Honest Communication

I'll tell you what's possible, what's not, and what I'd recommend — even if it means a smaller project. Trust beats revenue.

05

Partnership, Not Transaction

I'm invested in your success beyond the invoice. When your project thrives, I've done my job right.

06

Accessible by Default

Everything I build works for everyone — regardless of device, connection speed, or ability. Inclusivity isn't an add-on.
