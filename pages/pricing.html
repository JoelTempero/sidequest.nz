Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

What's included in the base price?

Every project includes custom design, responsive development, basic SEO setup, browser testing, and a handoff with documentation. I don't use templates — everything is built from scratch for you.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, I require a 50% deposit to begin work. The remaining 50% is due upon completion before the final files are delivered. This protects both of us.

What if I need changes after launch?

I offer a 30-day bug fix guarantee at no extra cost. For ongoing updates and maintenance, I offer monthly retainer packages starting at $200/month.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely. If the calculator shows more than you can afford, let's talk. I can often adjust scope or phase the project to fit your budget while still delivering something great.

Do you offer hosting?

I can set you up with hosting and manage it for you, or hand everything off to your own hosting provider. I'll recommend the best option based on your project needs.

How accurate is this estimate?

It's a ballpark figure. Most final quotes land within 20% of the estimate, but complex requirements or scope changes can affect the final price. The consultation call is free and gives you an exact number.
