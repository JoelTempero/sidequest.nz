My Living Hope

A custom Shopify theme for a store that builds community through faith-inspired products.

Building community, one product at a time

My Living Hope sells community-building products for Christian communities. Our custom Shopify theme includes fully customisable sections to share their story and an interactive widget where users can try out one of the products digitally — straight on the main page.

The goal was to create a store that feels warm and inviting, not just transactional. Every section was designed to tell a story, from the hero banner through to the product grid. The theme is fully manageable through Shopify's admin — the client can update content, images, and products without needing a developer.
