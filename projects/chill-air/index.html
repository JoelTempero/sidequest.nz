Chill Air

A strong digital home base for a one-man heat pump operation — professional, clear, and built to convert.

Client Greg Hicks
Type Website + Portal
Location Canterbury, NZ
Live at chillair.co.nz

Meet Greg

He's the owner-operator behind Chill Air, delivering reliable heating and cooling solutions with a friendly, no-fuss approach. Chill Air needed a website that felt professional, explained services clearly, and made it easy for customers to get in touch for quotes and bookings.

We designed and built chillair.co.nz as a clean, mobile-first site that prioritises clarity and conversion. Confident visual identity, straightforward service information, and clear calls-to-action throughout — with a simple enquiry flow so visitors can quickly understand what Chill Air offers and take the next step.

Behind the scenes, the site was structured with SEO-friendly page hierarchy and performance in mind, so it loads fast and reads well on any device.

What we built

  • Mobile-first responsive website
  • SEO-optimised page structure
  • Client portal for job management
  • Xero invoicing integration
  • Client progress updates
  • Calendar & scheduling system
  • Performance-tuned for fast loads

The Build

The result?

A site that works like a quiet salesperson 24/7 — building trust at first glance, turning more visitors into enquiries, and supporting long-term growth through search visibility.
